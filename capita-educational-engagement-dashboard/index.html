CAPITA Educational Engagement Dashboard

The core aim of our project is to produce a system that automatically gathers and processes engagement information about schools and colleges across the UK, from many different sources, such as Facebook or Twitter, in order to help education institutions to improve the education they provide. ---and other stuff
