CAPITA Educational Engagement Dashboard

The core aim of our project is to produce a system that automatically gathers and processes engagement information about schools and colleges across the UK, from many different sources, such as Facebook or Twitter, in order to help education institutions to improve the education they provide.

In order to achieve this, our team developed a powerful API that firstly manages to gather topic-related information from social media sources. In our case, the chosen topic is "educational institution", thus, we collect all the available information in all it's forms from various sources: Twitter (tweets), Facebook (events) and the most popular student forum in UK: The Student Room.

After we collect all the desired data related to our purpose (educational institutions), we run various advanced algorithms together with the purpose of gaining relevant insight that will be useful for our client: CAPITA - as their interest is to provide quality software for UK schools and universities.

Key Features

We are proud with our accomplishments. You can take a look at the the main features that we provide.

Topic-Related Data Capturing

Our system captures specific information from various sources: Facebook, Twitter, Student Forum

Interactive Dashboard

We consider that our system should not only be used by future developers. Every user can get desired insight over any educational institution throught our user friendly interface.

Accurate Analysis

In order to obtain valuable insight, our data is passed to powerful analysis algorithms such as: Named-Entity Recogniser, Natural Language Processing and Topic Modeling.

Data Storage

As we are working with big data, our developers are using PostgreSQL: the most advanced open-source database, as it is optimised for complex data models.
